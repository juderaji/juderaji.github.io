About Me :

I am an analyst with a background in both the technical and strategic aspects of data analysis and business intelligence. My career began with a Bachelors degree in Civil Engineering, where I refined my abilities leading me to specialize with a Diploma in Business Management. This combination of experiences has equipped me to approach data from both creative perspectives.

Throughout my journey I have achieved milestones, such as spearheading the development of an innovative CRM Launchpad and driving significant enhancements in reporting and operational efficiency for various client organizations. My skills encompass a variety of tools and technologies including proficiency in Power BI, Microsoft SQL and Data Visualization through Tableau and Power BI Dashboards.

Aside from working with numbers I am dedicated to learning and growth as demonstrated by my certifications, in Power BI Data Analysis and Google Analytics. When I'm not immersed in data analysis I enjoy exploring parts of the world staying active through sports activities and participating in gaming.

In this portfolio presentation I invite you to discover more about my background the projects that have motivated me creatively challenge me professionally offer insight into how I can contribute data solutions to your team or project.
